What is Navigator?

Navigator is a guided Q&A that helps you explore your interests, strengths, values, and work style — then suggests 8–12 realistic career ideas tailored to your answers.

It's designed to feel like a conversation with someone who cares about *your* fit, not a generic quiz. No wrong answers. Plenty of room for "not sure yet."

23 thoughtful questions covering interests, strengths, values, work style, skills, and lifestyle.

Personalized results grouped into "Strong fits," "Worth exploring," and "Wildcard" ideas.

Concrete next steps to explore 2–3 paths in the next month.

15–20 minutes to complete. Works in Claude or ChatGPT.
